About Max Faneuff

Max Faneuff is a full-stack web developer, who studied a MERN Stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, NodeJS) at the UNC Coding Bootcamp. Max is a hard-working, detail-orientated, passionate programmer, eager to add value to any product. Max is proficient in numerous coding languages, and brings an eye for style and seamless functionality to any project

Previously, Max spent nearly a decade in the Broadcast News industry, directing a team of broadcast professionals daily during live-to-air newscast productions. Max has developed unparalleled leadership abilities, mastering the interpersonal, multitasking, and communication skills necessary to meet deadlines with the highest-possible quality. Max is eager to translate those leadership abilites to the technology industry.
